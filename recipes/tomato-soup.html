Tomato Soup

Description

Nothing is as cozy as a good and rich tomato soup. Perfect for those rainy days, when we need an embrace. Don't forget the grilled cheeese sandwich on the side.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg Roma tomatoes
  • 42 gr unsalted butter
  • 4 cloves gralic, grated.
  • 2 shallots, finely chopped
  • 1 sprig sage
  • 500-750 ml chicken stock
  • 125 ml heavy whipping cream (a little extra, for serving)
  • salt and pepper, to taste
  • finely chopped chives or basil leaves

Steps

  1. Preheat oven at 200Â°C. Halve the tomatoes and place them cut-side down on a baking sheet and roast them for 30 minutes or until charred and soft.
  2. In a large saucespan, melt the butter and add shallots and garlic and cook at medium heat until soft, watch out, it can burn if unatended. Add the tomatoes, stock and sage, bring to a boil, then simmer for 30 minutes or until the mixture is very soft.
  3. Remove the sage, mix in the heavy cream and season with salt and pepper. Transfer to a blender and pulse until desired consistency (velvety smooth or with little chunks).
  4. Serve in bowls while still hot and drizzle in heavy cream, olive oil and chives or basil leaves.
